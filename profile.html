About Me

I am currently an MSQPC student at the University of Wisconsin Madison. I work with Prof. Micheline Soley on variational quantum eigensolver and quantum algorithms. More specifically, we utilize and investigate different optimization techniques in minimizing the energy and finding the best ground-state energy of a particle in an unknown potential. My prior research interests include the application of neural networks in computer vision and quantum error correction. I am particularly interested in deploying and investigating machine learning and optimization techniques in the context of a decoder for Hypergraph-product code. Furthermore, I am also interested in semi and unsupervised learning, representation learning, and generative models.
